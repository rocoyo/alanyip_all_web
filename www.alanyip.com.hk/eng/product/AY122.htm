Sushi Clock

Model No. AY122

 

 

The Sushi Clock is the first of its kind in the world, which provides bendable EL time display. The end user can change the curve of the EL display to fit his/her own taste. The light up digits provide good visibility in both bright and dark environments. The Sushi Clock is powered by a DC adaptor at home; and it can be served as a travel clock powered by battery.

 
Patented Design
Size: L200 x W100 x D68 mm
Housing material: Polypropylene
Battery: 2A x 2 pieces
